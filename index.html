About Hridyarambh Clinic

Hridyarambh Clinic & Pharmacy is a trusted polyclinic located at Makwana Road, Vasant Centria, Marol Naka, Andheri East, Mumbai. We are committed to delivering comprehensive and compassionate healthcare under one roof.

Our clinic is led by experienced specialists including Dr. Kiran Narang (Cardiologist) and Dr. Pallavi Chauhan (Gynaecologist), supported by a team of visiting consultants across multiple specialties. From preventive care to advanced diagnostics, we aim to provide accurate, timely, and patient-focused treatment.

With modern diagnostic facilities such as 2D Echo, TMT (Stress Test), Blood Tests, Sonography, and Scanning, along with an in-house pharmacy, we ensure convenience and continuity of care for every patient.

We also offer consultation services in Orthopaedics, Paediatrics, Diabetology, Urology, and General Physician care through scheduled appointments.
